About — the photographer

Hello, I'm Loumicha.

I live off-grid on a small homestead in the Kootenays — keeping a sprawling herb garden, making art in every medium I can get my hands on, and spending most golden hours with a camera.

“ My work sits somewhere between portrait and field‑note — chasing the moment after the laugh, the breath before the kiss, the way evening light moves across someone's hand.

Photography found me through the slow craft of paying attention. Fluent in both English and French, I love connecting with people in the language closest to their heart. When I'm not behind the lens you'll find me in the garden, foraging, or creating something with my hands.

Most of all, I want you to feel at home in front of me. That's the gift I bring: a softness that lets you exhale, so the real picture can show up on its own.

For founders & makers

Photographs that tell the whole story.

For small brands, I shoot the product alongside the maker — the hands that mixed it, the kitchen it was born in, the light it grew up in. The result feels like a love letter to your craft, not a catalogue page.

We start with a slow morning visit, walk your space together, and shape the day around the rhythm of natural light.
